Kurkure

Kurkure is a brand of corn puffs produced and marketed by PepsiCo, and first manufactured in India in 1999. Kurkure is manufactured and sold mainly in India; it is also available in Pakistan and Bangladesh. Fritolay Canada also manufacturers Kurkure locally.
